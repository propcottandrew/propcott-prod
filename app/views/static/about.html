    What is Propcott.com?

    Propcott.com aims to provide any discontent indvidual with the platform to start or join a boycotting campaign with unparalleled ease.

    What are Propcotts?

    Propcotts are the reinventions of boycotts. By grasping the very fundamental principle of boycotts- to combine and abstain from a given entity, propcotts simplify the strenuous process of initiating a boycott by breaking down the essential aspects into a few easy-to-understand sections (Who, What, Why, How). In doing so, individuals who are seeking to start a boycott propcott can get the campaign off the ground within a matter of minutes while those considering joining can quickly understand what the propcott is all about.

    How to Propcott?

    • Start a Propcott Campaign by clicking "Start a Propcott" at the top of the page and filling out the five fields listed.
    • Join a Propcott by clicking "Join" on the left side of the page after reading through the propcott.
